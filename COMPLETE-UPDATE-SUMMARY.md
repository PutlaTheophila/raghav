## How It Works:

### Viewing Individual Profiles:

1. **Go to People Page:**
   - Navigate to `/views/people.html`

2. **Browse Team Members:**
   - See all current members and alumni
   - Each card shows: photo, name, degree, education, research focus, description

3. **View Full Profile:**
   - Click "View Full Profile" button on any member
   - Opens dedicated profile page with complete information
   - Includes:
     - Large profile photo
     - Full biography
     - Complete education history
     - Research interests
     - Publications (if available)
     - Contact email (if available)

4. **Navigate Back:**
   - Click "Back to People" button
   - Returns to main team page
