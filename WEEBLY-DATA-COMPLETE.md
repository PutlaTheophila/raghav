## 🔬 Research Focus

### Thrust 1: Photo-chemistry and Molecular Actuators
- Solid-state photochemistry using [2+2] and [4+4] cycloaddition
- **Photosalient effect** - converting light to mechanical motion
- Light-driven flagella-like crystal motion
- Molecular photoactuators development

### Thrust 2: Battery Materials
- Metal-ion batteries (Li-ion, Na-ion)
- Degradation mechanism studies
- Anode materials for improved lifespan
- Applications: EVs and power storage

### Key Research Areas:
- Inorganic and metal-organic materials
- Crystalline materials design
- Energy storage solutions
- Nonlinear optics
- Multi-photon absorption
- Lasing materials
