## Research Focus

The laboratory concentrates on addressing global challenges through materials science and photochemistry. Key areas include:

- Development of crystalline materials (inorganic, organic, and metal-organic compounds)
- Applications in photochemistry, semiconducting materials, and energy solutions
- Emphasis on "solid-state materials and devices for light-driven micromachines, photo-physical and nonlinear optics"
